Unimost was founded by MOST Enerji’s Electric, Electronic,Professional Network Techlonogies, Telecomminication and Machine Equipments experts and ŞALT Enerji’s Endustrial Facilities and Renewable Energy experts to monitor, manage and care for Solar Energy Panels maintance all across Turkey. In order to get the maximum efficeny out of Solar Energy Panels, these panels should to be supervised constantly and should be fixed immediately in the case of a breakdown.So our purpose is to supervise these solar panels by professional softwares and take care of the crisis that might occur.In supervising these panels, like most companies, we use “INAVITAS” GES software produced by ENDOKS.

Our Mission 
In Unimost energy solutions company, we use the motto “Anytime, Anywhere, Uninterrupted Energy”. So we took it upon ourselves to raise awareness about renewable energy (especially solar energy) with our services.

Our Vision
To use the renewable energy sources to benefit sustainable development and to provide customer satisfaction without condition thus creating our identity.

Environmental Policy

-Our primary goal is to create a healthy and safe working environment, to organize necessary training activities to bring and develop this awareness to all our employees
- Developing environmentally friendly products and services
- To strictly take into account the environmental effects in the business process as an integrity related to our work without compromising
- To prevent any kind of environmental pollution that may occur during the process we are working on
-To improve efforts to conserve our energy resources
-To comply with all legal requirements regarding Environmental Issues and Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) and
to improve its continuity by making it a part of its culture
- With a systematic approach, predetermining the dangers related to OHS in the establishment,
to take all preventive measures before their diseases occur,
- To present our customer energy solutions in the most beneficial and environmentally friendly way
-To ensure continuous improvement by following the OHS and Environmental Management Systems,
As Unimost Energy, we are committed to all of our customers.
